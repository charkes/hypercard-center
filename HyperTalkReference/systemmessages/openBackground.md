# openBackground

<code><pre>
openBackground
</pre></code>

Handler:

<code><pre>
on openBackground
  [ph:statements]
end openBackground
</pre></code>

HyperCard sends the <code>openBackground</code> message to the current card just after a user (or script) goes to a card whose background differs from the background of the most recent card.
