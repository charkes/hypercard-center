# closeCard

`closeCard`

Handler:

<code><pre>
on closeCard
   <i>statements</i>
end closeCard
</pre></code>


HyperCard sends the<code> closeCard </code>message to a card when a user (or script) goes to another card; deletes a card, background, or stack; or quits HyperCard.
