### formatName

One of the following names of a date or time format:

<code><pre>
seconds
dateItems
[[ph:adjective]] date
[[ph:adjective]] time
</pre></code>

For example:

<code><pre>
abbr date
abbr time
abbrev date
abbrev time
abbreviated date
abbreviated time
dateItems
long date
long time
seconds
short date
short time
</pre></code>
